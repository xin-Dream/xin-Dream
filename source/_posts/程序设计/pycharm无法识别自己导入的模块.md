解决：同级目录中自己写的文件，在其他文件中不能调用

1. File->settings->Build,Excution,Deployment->Console->Python Console
2. 勾选

```
Add content roots to PYTHONPATH
Add source roots to PYTHONPATH
```

3. 在项目上右键->Mark Diectory as ->source roots
